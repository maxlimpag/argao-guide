Getting to and around Argao

Cebu City to Argao and vice versa

By bus/v-hire
Ceres, Sunrays, RoughRider and V-Hire
Buses and public vans leave regularly for Argao from the South Bus Terminal on N. Bacalso Avenue in Cebu City. From Argao, tourists can ride on buses that pass by the town on their way to Cebu City. V-hire units also ply the Argao-Cebu City route.

Argao to Carcar City
Carcar is separated from Argao by the town of Sibonga. Transportation to Carcar from the town is through buses or multicabs plying the route.

Within Argao
Modes of transport to get from one place to another in Argao include the habal-habal or motorcycles-for-hire for mountainous routes and short distances and tricycle for areas within the town center.
